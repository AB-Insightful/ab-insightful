# Getting Started with AB Insightful

Welcome to AB Insightful! This guide will help you get up and running with your first A/B test. AB Insightful is a Shopify app that lets you run experiments on your store to optimize conversions and make data-driven decisions.

## What is AB Insightful?

AB Insightful helps you run A/B tests (also called split tests) on your Shopify store. You can test different versions of your store—such as different headlines, layouts, or product sections—to see which performs better with your customers. The app collects data, runs statistical analysis, and gives you clear recommendations on when to deploy a winning variant.

## Navigating the App

The main navigation bar appears at the top of the app and includes the following sections:

- **Home** — Your dashboard with a quick overview of your most recent experiment, setup status, and key metrics.
- **Experiments** — View and manage all your A/B experiments in one place.
- **Create Experiment** — Start a new experiment from scratch.
- **Reports** — Access experiment reports, session data, and conversion metrics.
- **Help** — Find guides and documentation (like this one).
- **Settings** — Configure app-wide settings.

## Major Sections Overview

### Home

The Home page is your central hub. It shows:

- **Mandatory Setup** — If you haven’t completed setup, you’ll see steps to enable tracking and verify the app embed on your theme. These steps are required before experiments can collect data.
- **Latest Experiment** — A summary of your most recent experiment, including variant performance and key metrics.
- **Charts** — Probability of being best and expected loss over time for your latest experiment.

### Experiments

The Experiments page lists all your experiments. You can:

- See experiment status (Draft, Active, Paused, Completed, Archived)
- Start, pause, resume, end, or archive experiments
- Rename experiments
- Navigate to individual experiment details or reports

### Create Experiment

Use this page to create a new A/B test. You’ll define:

- Experiment name and description
- Control and variant section IDs (from your theme)
- Conversion goals
- Traffic allocation
- Start date and end conditions (e.g., end date or stable success probability)

### Reports

The Reports section provides:

- **Reports Overview** — A summary table of all experiments with status, run length, end conditions, and conversions. Click an experiment name to view its detailed report.
- **Sessions & Conversions** — Aggregate metrics for your store, filterable by date range.
- **Individual Experiment Reports** — Deep-dive into each experiment with variant comparison tables, charts, and recommendations.

### Settings

Configure app-wide options and preferences.

## Next Steps

1. **Complete Mandatory Setup** — Enable tracking and verify the app embed on your theme (see the Home page).
2. **Create your first experiment** — Go to Create Experiment and follow the form to set up a test.
3. **Review reports** — After your experiment runs, check the Reports section to see results and recommendations.

For more detailed guides, see **Creating & Managing Experiments**, **Viewing Reports**, and **Understanding Your Results**.
